USE CASE 1 — On-Field Coaching & Training Support

File: U-18 Warm-Up Drills
Category: On-field coaching / training
Team Level: U-18

1) Dynamic Warm-Up Circuit (10–12 minutes)
- Objective: Raise core temperature, activate key muscle groups, prepare for high-intensity work.
- Suitable for: U-16 to U-18
- Equipment: Cones, balls
- Steps:
  a) Jog & mobility (2 min)
  b) High-knees, butt kicks, lateral shuffles (3 min)
  c) Open/close the gate hip mobility (2 min)
  d) Short accelerations 10–15m at 60–80% intensity (3–5 reps)

2) Position-Specific Rondo Warm-Up (8–10 minutes)
- Objective: First touch, scanning, quick decisions.
- Suitable for: U-18, senior
- Format: 5v2 or 6v2 with neutral support
- Coaching points:
  - One-touch when possible
  - Always check shoulder before receiving
  - Use 2-touch maximum under pressure

3) U-18 Pre-Match Warm-Up Template (20 minutes)
- Minute 0–5: Light jog & mobility across half-pitch
- Minute 5–10: Dynamic stretching (hips, hamstrings, calves)
- Minute 10–15: Passing patterns (2-touch passing in pairs, then 3rd-man runs)
- Minute 15–18: Small-sided rondo (6v2) in tight area
- Minute 18–20: 2–3 short sprints (10–20m) at 90% intensity
